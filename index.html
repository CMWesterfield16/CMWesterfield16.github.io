Caitlin Westerfield

Hi, my name is Caitlin and I am a software engineer at the Chan Zuckerburg Initiative on the Education Initiative's Learning Platform Team. I am particularly interested in using technology to address equity gaps within the education system and guide policy decision-making.

I am a 2020 graduate of Yale University with a degree in computer science and religious studies. After graduation, I lived in Portland, ME, while working for the Sara Gideon for Maine 2020 Senate Campaign.

Please feel free to send me an email at cmwesterfield@gmail.com. I would love to hear from you!
